Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform, security, and enterprise plans.

What kind of security features do you offer? add

Our platform includes enterprise-grade security features such as Single Sign-On (SSO), SCIM user provisioning, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and detailed audit logs. We are compliant with major standards like SOC 2 and GDPR.

How do you handle data privacy and compliance? add

Data privacy is a top priority. We adhere to strict data protection protocols and are fully compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and other regional regulations. Our infrastructure is hosted on secure, certified cloud providers, and data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Can Acme integrate with our existing tools? add

Yes. We offer a robust API and have pre-built integrations with many popular enterprise systems, including CRMs, ERPs, and identity providers. Our professional services team can also assist with custom integration projects.

What does the implementation process look like? add

Each enterprise client is assigned a dedicated account manager and an onboarding specialist. We work with you to develop a customized implementation plan, which includes configuration, data migration, team training, and go-live support to ensure a smooth transition.
